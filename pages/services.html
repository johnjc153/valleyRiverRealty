How The Custom Seattle Home Process Works

A part of our firm dedication to amazing customer care involves keeping you in the loop every step of the way. Having a custom home builder in Seattle involves a lot of steps, questions, and potential concerns. Trusting the project to an experienced team is the first step to a great outcome. Understanding the process helps, too.
1) Meet with our Seattle custom home builder experts to discuss your property, plan, budget, and more. We will take the time to explain everything in detail, make recommendations, and take all of your needs and dreams into account.
2) The architectural plans, blueprints, budget and finance details, and timeline are finalized. We ensure you accept everything before we break ground for the first time.
3) Construction begins. We keep you updated on every part of the project and welcome questions or comments about your Seattle custom home. Our goal goes beyond your satisfaction. We make it our mission to create a house you will be proud to call your own for many years to come.
